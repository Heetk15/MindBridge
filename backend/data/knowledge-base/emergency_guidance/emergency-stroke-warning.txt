# Recognizing a Stroke: FAST

## The FAST Protocol
A stroke is a medical emergency. Use the FAST test:
- **F**ace: Ask the person to smile. Does one side of the face droop?
- **A**rms: Ask them to raise both arms. Does one arm drift downward?
- **S**peech: Ask them to repeat a simple phrase. Is their speech slurred or strange?
- **T**ime: If you observe any of these signs, call 911 immediately.

## Other Symptoms
- Sudden numbness or weakness in the face, arm, or leg.
- Sudden trouble seeing in one or both eyes.
- Sudden severe headache with no known cause.
- Sudden trouble walking, dizziness, loss of balance.

## Critical Action
Note the time when symptoms first appeared. This is crucial for emergency responders.
