# Managing Medications Safely

## Organization
- Use a pill organizer, divided by days and times.
- Keep a detailed, up-to-date medication list, including dosages and purposes.
- Store medications in a safe, dry place away from children and confused adults.

## Tracking and Refills
- Set alarms or use reminder apps to ensure timely administration.
- Schedule refills well in advance so you never run out of essential medications.
- Regularly review the medication list with a pharmacist or doctor.
