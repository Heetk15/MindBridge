# Having Difficult Conversations

## Driving Safety
When it's time to stop driving:
- Approach the topic with empathy, not accusation.
- Focus on safety, using statements like "I am worried about your safety on the road."
- Offer alternatives, such as driving them yourself or arranging transportation services.

## Moving to Assisted Living
- Start the conversation early, before a crisis occurs.
- Highlight the benefits, such as social activities and no home maintenance.
- Involve them in the decision-making process as much as possible.
