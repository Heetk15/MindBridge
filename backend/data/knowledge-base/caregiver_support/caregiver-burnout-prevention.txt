# Preventing Caregiver Burnout

## Recognizing Burnout
Signs of burnout include constant exhaustion, irritability, sleep issues, and feeling a loss of purpose. Recognizing these signs early is crucial.

## Self-Care Strategies
- Schedule daily "me time," even if it's just 15 minutes.
- Engage in regular physical activity and maintain a healthy diet.
- Practice mindfulness or meditation.

## Seeking Help
- Do not hesitate to ask family members for assistance.
- Consider professional respite care services.
- Join a caregiver support group to share experiences and receive emotional support.
