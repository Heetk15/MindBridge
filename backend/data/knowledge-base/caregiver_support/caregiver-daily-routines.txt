# Establishing Daily Routines

## The Importance of Routine
A predictable routine reduces anxiety for the person receiving care and makes the caregiver's day more manageable.

## Creating a Schedule
- Wake up and go to bed at the same time every day.
- Schedule meals, medications, and activities at consistent times.
- Build in buffer times for unexpected delays.

## Flexibility
While routine is important, be prepared to adapt if the person is having a bad day. Rigidity can lead to frustration.
