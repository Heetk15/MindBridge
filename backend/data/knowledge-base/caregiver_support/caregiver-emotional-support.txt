# Finding Emotional Support

## Emotional Challenges
Caregiving can bring a mix of emotions: love, resentment, guilt, and grief. It is completely normal to experience these conflicting feelings.

## Building a Support Network
- Talk to friends who understand or are willing to listen without judgment.
- Connect with other caregivers online or in local support groups.
- Consider individual therapy or counseling to process complex emotions.

## Forgiving Yourself
You will make mistakes. Forgive yourself and recognize that you are doing the best you can in a difficult situation.
