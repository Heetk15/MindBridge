# How to Use the SOS Feature

## What is the SOS Button?
The SOS button is a large, red emergency button located on the home screen of the MindBridge app. It is designed for immediate assistance in critical situations.

## Activating SOS
1. Press and hold the red SOS button for 3 seconds.
2. The app will immediately send an emergency alert (SMS and push notification) to all designated primary caregivers.
3. It will also display a prompt asking if you want to dial 911 directly from your device.

## False Alarms
If you press the button by mistake, you have a 10-second window to tap "Cancel" before the alerts are dispatched.
