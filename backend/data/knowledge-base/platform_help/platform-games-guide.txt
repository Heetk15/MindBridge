# Using MindBridge Cognitive Games

## Accessing Games
Go to the "Cognitive Health" or "Games" section from the main menu. Here you will find a selection of games designed to improve memory, attention, and problem-solving.

## Recommended Usage
- We recommend playing for 15-20 minutes a day.
- Start with the "Beginner" levels and progress as you feel comfortable.
- The platform tracks your progress over time, which you can view in the "My Stats" area.

## Types of Games
- **Memory Match:** Improves short-term memory.
- **Word Search:** Enhances vocabulary and pattern recognition.
- **Daily Trivia:** Stimulates recall and general knowledge.
