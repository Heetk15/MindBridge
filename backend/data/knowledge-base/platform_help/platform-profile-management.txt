# Managing Your MindBridge Profile

## Updating Medical Information
It is crucial to keep medical information up to date for emergency purposes.
1. Go to "Profile" -> "Medical Info".
2. Update current medications, allergies, and primary physician contact details.
3. Save changes.

## Changing Emergency Contacts
1. Navigate to "Profile" -> "Emergency Contacts".
2. You can add up to 5 contacts. Ensure the primary contact is listed first, as they will receive the initial SOS alerts.
3. Verify phone numbers regularly to ensure alerts are delivered correctly.
