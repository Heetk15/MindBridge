# Cognitive Stimulation for the Elderly

## Neuroplasticity
The brain can continue to form new connections throughout life. Engaging in new and challenging activities promotes this.

## Brain-Boosting Activities
- Learn a new language or how to play a musical instrument.
- Engage in complex crafts like knitting or woodworking.
- Read books or articles on unfamiliar subjects.
- Play strategic games like chess, bridge, or sudoku.

## Everyday Challenges
Break routines to challenge the brain: take a different route when walking, or use your non-dominant hand for simple tasks like brushing your teeth.
