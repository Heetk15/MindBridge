# Senior Nutrition Guidelines

## Essential Nutrients
- **Calcium and Vitamin D:** Crucial for bone health. Sources include fortified milk, yogurt, and leafy greens.
- **Vitamin B12:** Important for nerve function. Found in meat, fish, poultry, and fortified cereals.
- **Fiber:** Helps prevent constipation. Sources include whole grains, beans, fruits, and vegetables.

## Overcoming Poor Appetite
- Serve smaller, more frequent meals instead of three large ones.
- Enhance flavor with herbs and spices rather than salt.
- Ensure the dining environment is pleasant and social.

## Hydration
Seniors should aim for 6-8 glasses of fluid a day, unless restricted by a doctor. Soups, fruits, and vegetables also contribute to fluid intake.
