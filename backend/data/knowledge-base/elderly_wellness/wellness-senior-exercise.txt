# Exercise for Seniors

## Types of Exercise
- **Cardiovascular:** Walking, swimming, or stationary cycling improves heart health. Aim for 150 minutes a week.
- **Strength Training:** Using light weights or resistance bands helps maintain muscle mass and bone density.
- **Flexibility:** Stretching exercises like yoga keep joints mobile.
- **Balance:** Tai chi or standing on one foot near a sturdy chair helps prevent falls.

## Getting Started Safely
- Always consult a physician before starting a new exercise regimen.
- Start with 5-10 minute sessions and gradually increase.
- Listen to your body; stop if you feel pain, dizziness, or shortness of breath.
