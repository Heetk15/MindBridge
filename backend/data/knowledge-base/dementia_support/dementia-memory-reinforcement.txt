# Memory Reinforcement Techniques

## Using Visual Aids
Labeling drawers, cupboards, and doors with words or pictures can help individuals find what they need independently. 

## Reminiscence Therapy
- Look through old photo albums together.
- Discuss positive past events without pressing them to remember specific details.
- Play music from their youth.

## Memory Stations
Create a dedicated space with items related to their past hobbies or professions. This can provide comfort and stimulate positive memories.
