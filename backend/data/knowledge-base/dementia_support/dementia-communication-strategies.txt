# Communication Strategies for Dementia

## Keep It Simple
Use short, simple sentences. Ask one question at a time and wait for a response.

## Non-Verbal Communication
- Maintain eye contact and stay at their eye level.
- Use hand gestures to demonstrate what you mean.
- Ensure your facial expressions are relaxed and friendly.

## Patience is Key
Give the person ample time to process information and respond. Avoid interrupting or finishing their sentences for them.
