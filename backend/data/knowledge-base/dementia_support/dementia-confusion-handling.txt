# Handling Dementia Confusion

## Understanding the Cause
Confusion in dementia patients can be triggered by changes in routine, environment, or medication. It is important to approach the situation with calmness and patience.

## De-escalation Techniques
- Do not argue or try to correct the person.
- Validate their feelings by saying, "I understand you are feeling confused right now."
- Gently redirect their attention to a familiar and calming activity.
- Ensure the environment is well-lit and free of loud noises.

## Prevention Strategies
- Maintain a consistent daily routine.
- Use visual cues, like large clocks and calendars.
- Keep the living space organized and clutter-free.
