# Addressing Wandering Behavior

## Why Wandering Happens
Wandering is common in mid-to-late stage dementia. It can be caused by restlessness, a desire to fulfill a past obligation (like "going to work"), or searching for a familiar place.

## Safety Measures
- Install alarms on doors and windows.
- Ensure the person wears a medical ID bracelet or a GPS tracker.
- Keep a recent photograph and medical information ready in case they go missing.

## Safe Exploration
- Provide a safe, enclosed area for them to walk, such as a fenced garden.
- Accompany them on daily walks to reduce pent-up energy.
