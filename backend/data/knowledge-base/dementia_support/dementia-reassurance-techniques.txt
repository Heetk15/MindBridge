# Reassurance Techniques

## Validation Therapy
Acknowledge the person's reality. If they are looking for a deceased spouse, instead of saying the spouse has passed away, ask them to tell you about the spouse.

## Physical Comfort
A gentle touch, holding hands, or a light hug can provide significant comfort and ground the person.

## Reassuring Phrases
- "You are safe here."
- "I am here with you."
- "We will figure this out together."
