# Strategies for Tracking Medication

## Visual Reminders
Place the pillbox in a highly visible location, such as the kitchen table, where it will be seen at meal times.

## Technology Aids
- Use smart pillboxes that light up or beep when it is time for a dose.
- Set recurring alarms on a smartphone or smart speaker.
- Use the MindBridge app's medication reminder feature.

## Routine Integration
Tie medication administration to an existing daily habit, such as brushing teeth or drinking morning coffee.
