# General Medication Safety

## Understanding the Prescription
Always read the label carefully. Understand whether the medication should be taken with food, on an empty stomach, or at a specific time of day.

## Avoiding Interactions
- Provide all doctors with a comprehensive list of medications, including over-the-counter drugs and supplements.
- Use one primary pharmacy so they can check for potential drug interactions.
- Avoid mixing medications with alcohol.

## Proper Disposal
Do not flush old or unused medications down the toilet. Use community drug take-back programs or mix them with undesirable substances (like coffee grounds) before throwing them in the trash.
